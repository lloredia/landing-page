Core Features

Everything Nightlife
Runs On.

Night List replaces the DMs, the phone calls, the paper guest lists, and the guesswork — with one platform built for how clubs actually operate.

🗺️
Interactive Floor Plan

Guests see the actual layout of your venue. Tap a table, see the details. The visual booking experience no one else has.

⚡
Real-Time Availability

Tables update live. No double bookings, no back-and-forth. Guests always see what's actually open tonight.

🎤
Promoter Management

Create sub-accounts for your promoters. Track their bookings, measure their performance, and pay commissions automatically.

🎁
Promo Engine

Set promos per table — "4 people in free", "complimentary champagne", "2-for-1 mixers". Guests see it before they book.

💰
Dynamic Pricing

Different prices for Friday vs Saturday. Event-based pricing overrides. Minimum spend by table type. Full control.

📈
Revenue Analytics

See projected revenue, occupancy rates, peak hours, and promoter ROI. Data your venue has never had before.
