Core Features

Everything Nightlife
Runs On.

Night List replaces the DMs, phone calls, paper guest lists, and guesswork — with one platform built for how clubs actually operate.

🗺️
Interactive Floor Plan

Guests see the actual layout of your venue. Tap a table, see the details. The visual booking experience no one else in nightlife has built.

⚡
Real-Time Availability

Tables update live. No double bookings, no back-and-forth. Guests always see what's actually open tonight — not what was open this morning.

🎤
Promoter Management

Create sub-accounts for your promoters. Track their bookings, measure performance, and handle commissions — all inside the platform.

🎁
Promo Engine

Set promos per table — "4 guests in free", "complimentary champagne", "2-for-1 mixers". Guests see the deal before they commit.

💰
Dynamic Pricing

Different prices for Friday vs Saturday. Event-based overrides. Minimum spend by table type. Full pricing control in your hands.

📈
Revenue Analytics

Projected revenue, occupancy rates, peak hours, and promoter ROI — data your venue has never had access to before Night List.
